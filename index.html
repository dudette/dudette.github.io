Noir

Interactive gameboards, Arduino

Noir is a interactive game board where the digital part was programmed with Arduino. By using the digital elements in the game you can block your opponent and as well change the game board's layout. The constant change of the board's layout gives a variety of new ways of player experiences. Move walls and find your key to get into the chamber where the diamond is and at the same time block your opponent.
